About Me

My name is Jacob Peterson. I'm a 19 year old drummer and hobbyist developer from Riverton, Utah.

Outside of school, I spend most of my time practicing both the drums and web/game development.

I started programming small games using Game Maker 7 when I was just 8 years old. From there my interests only grew, and now I'm looking to make a full-time living in web development, and keep writing games on the side as a hobby.

I began playing drums for real when I was 15 after an intense session of Rock Band with my brother. I've since bought a few kits and have learned many songs, and I hope to start a band someday with my friends.

My favorite genres of music to play and listen to include all forms of Metal, Jazz, and Rock.
